About me

My name is Mateusz Knol.

I am a traveler, translator and tour leader. I obtained my tour guide license in 2000.

I am fluent in English, French, Spanish, Italian and Arabic. I know Portuguese and Russian on a communicative level.

Knowledge of foreign languages really opens up many cognitive paths. I consider this skill to be my greatest asset.

I travel around the world with a special love for Arab countries, Latin America, the Middle and Far East.

Beginnings

I graduated in Romance philology at the University of Lodz and translation techniques at the Marc Bloc University in Strasbourg. I also completed a post-graduate MBA (University of Lodz / University Of Maryland).

I started working abroad quite early - during my studies. From 1996 I started spending my holidays in the USA working with young people from all over the world. I spent the money I earned on traveling - initially around the USA, and then to many interesting countries around the world.

Soon I completed a course of tourism guides. And it hapenned. It started with residencies in the Canary Islands, mainland Spain, the Greek Islands and Morocco, where I worked for 5 years.

Experience

Since 2000, I have been professionally leading tourist groups with which I have reached over 120 countries around the world. I started my adventure in tourism as a youth tutor in the USA, a resident in Spain, the Canary Islands, Greece and Morocco, spending over 5 years abroad. Then I led tramping groups and exotic trips for individual tourists.

Since 2005, corporate employees have become more and more frequent guests on my trips. In the most active years of my work as a pilot, I spent over 300 days a year with groups on trips abroad around the world. In my career, I have led over four hundred groups for over 20 travel agencies and over 70 corporate clients.

Today I specialize in individual VIP groups and incentive trips. I am invited to cooperate with the most prestigious events on the market and there is practically no industry for which I would not conduct trips.

Hobbies

I grew up in a house of artists, in the spirit of freelance professions. Father Kazimierz Knol was a soloist and the first dancer of the ballet of the Grand Theater in Łódź. Mother Urszula Klechta-Knol is a painter: http://urszulaklechta.pl. Although Dad never taught me much, I became interested in music and dance myself. As luck would have it, the Cuban "tambor", African "djembe" and Caribbean dances spoke to me the most.

One of my many passions is sport. As a young boy, until the senior age, I was a player of the first-league water polo team of Anilana in Łódź. I got to know the taste of hard work, many successes and many failures - this is what sport teaches.

I am fascinated by climbing and mountains. There I find my personal "spirit of inspiration".

Thanks to my wife, Agnieszka, I became interested in spirituality and philosophical and religious systems of the East: Buddhism, Hinduism and Sufism. All in all, I believe that the greatest challenge for a person is to know "yourself".
